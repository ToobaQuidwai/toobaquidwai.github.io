Research Portfolio

Decoding Cilia — from molecular architecture to disease

Biomedical scientist with over a decade of international research experience across India, the UK, Germany, and the USA. Expertise in ciliary biology, vesicular trafficking, proteomics, genomics, and advanced imaging (super-resolution microscopy, CLEM, 3D-TEM, STORM, SIM, FRAP, FRET). Focused on understanding defective cilia-associated disease mechanisms — from kidney cysts and obesity to blindness and cancer. Published in eLife, AJHG, and Chemical Science, with 258 total citations.
